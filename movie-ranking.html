My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

Story of a three college students about thier struggles, it is a once in a life time movie that has no other substitute and it will take you to a roller coaster journey to make you laugh and cry.
*IMDb Rating - 8.4/10.
*Available on Amazon Prime.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara - ZNMD

Based on a trip of 3 friends, they take vacation in Spain, Costa Brava, San Fermin Festival. Kind of similar to Hangover. Meaning of the title is "you'll get life once".
*IMDb Rating - 8.2/10.
*Available on Netflix.

Sardar Udham Singh

Based on the massacre done by the britishers, after watching this movie you'll be thankfull for your life.
*IMDb Rating - 8.4/10
*Available on Amazon Prime.
